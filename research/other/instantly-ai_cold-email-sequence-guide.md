# Cold Email Sequence Guide
**Source:** Instantly.ai
**URL:** https://instantly.ai/blog/cold-email-sequence/
**Collected:** April 2026

## Summary
This guide provides a strategic framework for B2B cold email outreach in 2026, emphasizing precision targeting and personalization over volume. It includes four ready-to-use email sequence templates (Lead Generation, Referral, Sales Meeting Request, and Follow-Up) with 3-4 touchpoints spaced 2-4 business days apart, along with best practices for maintaining sender reputation and improving reply rates.

## Key Points & Strategies
- **Precision over volume:** Focus on ICP alignment and better targeting rather than high-volume blasts
- **Real personalization:** Each email should add value and reference specific observations about the prospect's company
- **Optimal sequence structure:** Use 3-4 touchpoints spaced 2-4 business days apart
- **Clear CTAs:** Make it easy for prospects to respond yes, no, or request follow-up later
- **Respect rejections:** Stop sequences after clear rejections to protect sender reputation
- **Value-first approach:** Lead with relevant case studies, resources, or insights before asking for meetings
- **Low-pressure tone:** Avoid sounding canned, desperate, or pushy; acknowledge when timing isn't right
- **Data-driven adjustments:** Use reply data to refine sequences over time
- **Multi-angle follow-ups:** Change your approach with each touchpoint to non-responders

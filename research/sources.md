# Research Sources — Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS

A curated list of 10 experts selected for their practical,
operator-level content on cold outreach, AI-powered personalization,
and B2B GTM workflows. Each expert was chosen based on the specificity
and recency of their content — not follower count.

---

4. **Aaron Shepherd** — https://www.youtube.com/@aaronxshepherd
   Outbound sales coach and B2B pipeline builder.

## Why These Experts

The selection covers every layer of a cold outreach pipeline:

- **Technical infrastructure** — Hans Dekker
  Emails need to land before anything else works.

- **AI and automation at scale** — Eric Nowoslawski, Nick Abraham
  Scale without losing personalization.

- **Copywriting and psychology** — Adam Erhart, Zahra Batool Butt
  The message itself drives replies.

- **Workflow and tooling** — Fernando Cao Zheng, Felix Frank, Lohit Boruah
  Systems that make outreach repeatable.

- **Pipeline and strategy** — Aaron Shepherd, Matt Lucero
  Turning outreach into measurable outcomes.

Together these sources are strong enough to support a full
cold outreach playbook for a B2B SaaS product.

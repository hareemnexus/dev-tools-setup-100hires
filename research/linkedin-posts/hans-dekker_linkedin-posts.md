## Post 1 — Email Infrastructure Setup

**Theme:** DNS authentication and sending infrastructure for cold email

Your emails aren't landing because your infrastructure isn't set up
right. It comes down to 3 things:

**1. DNS Authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)**
Think of it like a passport. Without those stamps, you look suspicious
at the border.

**2. Custom Tracking Domain**
Stop sharing reputation with everyone else. Own yours.

**3. Sending Settings**
Mimic human behavior. No blasting 200 emails at 9am sharp every day.

**Key insight:** Not flashy. But it's the reason your messages land.
Set it up once, rarely touch it again.

## Post 2 — Staying Out of Spam — The Real Game

**Theme:** Advanced deliverability beyond SPF/DKIM/DMARC

Most people think deliverability is about SPF, DKIM, and DMARC.
That's table stakes. That's not what separates good senders from
burned domains.

**The real game is what happens before your emails leave the system.**

Secure Email Gateways (SEGs) like Proofpoint, Mimecast, and Barracuda
sit between you and the inbox. They decide if your message ever
reaches a human. Instantly detects these at the lead level — you can
see which leads are protected and skip them entirely. No wasted sends.
No reputation damage.

**Behavioral layer filters:**
- Skip hostile prospects — filters out people statistically likely
  to mark you as spam or reply negatively
- Unlikely to reply — flags leads who consistently ignore outreach

**Key insight:** Think of it like a pre-flight check for every
campaign. You're not just cleaning bad data. You're protecting your
sender reputation before a single email goes out. Over time, these
filters compound. Your reputation stays clean while competitors burn
through domains wondering what went wrong.

## Post 3 — The 3 Eras of Cold Outreach

**Theme:** Evolution from manual to automation to AI agents

**Era 1: Manual**
Reps doing everything by hand. Research, writing, follow-ups.
It worked but didn't scale.

**Era 2: Automation**
Blast thousands of messages. Track opens. Schedule follow-ups.
Volume was easy. But relevance died. Inboxes flooded. Response
rates tanked.

**Era 3: AI Agents**
AI agents don't just automate tasks. They perform them with context.
They know who you're targeting. They know what your business does.
They know what conversations you're trying to start. Unlike humans,
they don't lose context, don't have off days, and don't take
vacations.

**Key insight:** The shift from automation to AI is the difference
between a machine that blindly follows instructions and a teammate
who understands the bigger picture. The future of cold outreach
won't be about sending more. It'll be about building a team of AI
agents that handle the repetitive work, keep full context, and free
you up to focus on strategy.

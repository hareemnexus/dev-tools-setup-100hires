## Summary

This video addresses a fundamental cold email mistake that goes beyond sequences and deliverability: misaligned positioning for cold traffic. Aaron Shepherd explains why strategies that work for inbound audiences fail in cold outreach and reveals how established businesses need to fundamentally rethink their cold email offers to match the cold traffic context.

## Key Points & Strategies

- **Cold vs. Inbound Positioning Gap** — What converts for warm/inbound audiences does NOT automatically work for cold email; requires completely different positioning
- **Cold-Traffic-Optimized Positioning** — Cold outreach demands a specific approach tailored to unfamiliar audiences, not a one-size-fits-all strategy
- **Rethink Your Offer** — Most established businesses fail because they present their offer the same way to cold prospects as they do to warm leads
- **Scale Matters** — Larger companies need to adjust their cold email approach to reflect their size and reputation, not use SMB tactics
- **Results Reflect Positioning** — Poor cold email conversion rates are usually a symptom of misaligned positioning, not sequence or technical issues
